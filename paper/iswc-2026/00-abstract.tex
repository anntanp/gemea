We present GeMeA, a publicly accessible knowledge graph over 23M+ objects from the German Digital Library (DDB), queryable via SPARQL (QLever), browsable via SHMARQL, and accessible to AI agents through an MCP interface — a combination not previously available for cultural heritage data at this scale. The resource includes a \texttt{mocho}-based ontology alignment layer mapping DDB EDM metadata to a domain- and modality-specific ontology, with partial GND entity linking via rule-based ISBD title extraction. GeMeA is released as a research testbed for work on ontology evaluation, agentic KG applications, RML-based declarative mapping, and provenance modelling of LLM-assisted enrichment.
